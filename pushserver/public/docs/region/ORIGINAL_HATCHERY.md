Welcome to (Neo)Habitat!
--------------------------
> *One of the really fun things you can do when you enter Habitat is customize your appearance. What you get to help you in this task is a customization program allowing you to make the kind of alterations that would make a Beverly Hills plastic surgeon cry.*
> 
> *You'll automatically be put into the customization program the first time you enter Habitat. Just follow the instructions it gives you and you'll do fine.*

*From the [Official Habitat Avatar Handbook](https://github.com/frandallfarmer/neohabitat-doc/blob/master/docs/historical/Habitat_Manual_1988.pdf), January 1988*

What you are seeing here is the first ever graphical character customization screen for customizing an avatar when entering the world. Almost all modern MMO services utilize a feature like this, but Habitat was the first to put it into practice back in 1987.

You haven't actually entered Habitat yet, you're in a special region we call a hatchery that is hosted locally in your client and allows you to customize your avatar as you see fit before you enter the world.
