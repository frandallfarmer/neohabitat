Welcome to (Neo)Habitat!
--------------------------
> *One of the really fun things you can do when you enter Habitat is customize your appearance. What you get to help you in this task is a customization program allowing you to make the kind of alterations that would make a Beverly Hills plastic surgeon cry.*
> 
> *You'll automatically be put into the customization program the first time you enter Habitat. Just follow the instructions it gives you and you'll do fine.*

*From the [Official Habitat Avatar Handbook](https://github.com/frandallfarmer/neohabitat-doc/blob/master/docs/historical/Habitat_Manual_1988.pdf), January 1988*

What you are seeing here is the first ever graphical character customization screen for customizing an avatar when logging in. Almost all modern MMO services utilize a feature like this, but Habitat was the first to put it into practice back in 1987.

You haven't actually entered NeoHabitat yet, you're in a special region we call a hatchery that is hosted locally in your client and allows you to customize your avatar as you see fit before you enter the world for real.

The following function keys are used to change your avatars appearance:

**F1** - *Changes your sex between Male or Female*

**F2** - *Changes the style of head you are wearing from a random selection of heads*

**F3** - *Changes the height of your avatar*

**F4** - *This option is meant to make your avatar walk around but is currently bugged*

**F5** - *Changes the pattern/color of your hairstyle*

**F6** - *This option is meant to change the pattern/color of your avatars legs but is currently bugged and will change your hair color*

**F7** - *Changes the pattern/color of your avatars torso*

**F8** - *Changes the pattern/color of your avatars arms*


Feel free to experiment with all of the available permutations available.

Your avatars name and appearance won't be committed to the NeoHabitat server ***until you press the space bar***, so if you don't like what you see, feel free to restart your Habitat client and try again.

If you are ready to continue, ***press the space bar*** and answer ***Y*** when the client asks if you are happy with your appearance. You'll then be whisked off to the NeoHabitat Immigration region and enter NeoHabitat for real as a fully fledged avatar.
